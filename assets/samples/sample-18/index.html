Design Process & Methodologies.

Who What When Where?

What is the problem, the opportunity? Who are the stakeholders? I begin with data derived from the client or other resources made avaliable.

After the initial Data is gathered, Project Scope (the story, direction, deliverables) takes shape via early Visuals (MAPS, MODELS, DIAGRAMS)

Contextual Research + Design Opportunities

I try to get out in the field as much as possible (physically present or via digital/tradition survey methods) to correct my assumptions, whether im observing or interacting with users and/or stakeholders.

Once the story is told, and the characters are present, I can bring ideas to life, through.. Sketching, Making, Moving, Thinking, Doing. Context is everything, and so is insight.

Prototyping & Testing + Final Visual Design

Test Early & Often + Build. Its impertive that concepts should meet reality, and the people that use it. Insight is golden, and is visible only in context.

Form and Function must maintain a balance, also an idea should be as beautiful as it is innovative. My process comes to an end based on each individual client & their parameters.
